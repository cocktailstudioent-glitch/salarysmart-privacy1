🎯 SalarySmart – Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026  |  Last updated: March 2026

✅ SalarySmart does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All your financial data stays on your device only.

1. Data We Collect

SalarySmart collects no personal data. All salary, expense, and goal information you enter is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (WebView localStorage) and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

2. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service by this app
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

3. Permissions We Use

  • INTERNET – Used only to load web fonts for the app interface. No user data is transmitted.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – Used to send a daily reminder to log your expenses. No data is included in notifications.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – Used to reschedule the daily notification after your device restarts.

4. Third-Party Services

SalarySmart does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs. No data is shared with any third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

SalarySmart is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@salarysmart.app
